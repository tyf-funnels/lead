Real Outcomes

These are outcomes based on the kind of work I do — realistic patterns when a site is built with the right intent.

// Example 01

14 Enquiries in 11 Days — Same Traffic

A 2-person consulting firm had consistent traffic but near-zero enquiries. After restructuring their main service page around a single clear offer and direct contact path, form submissions went from nothing to 14 in the first 11 days — no change to traffic, no ad spend.

// Example 02

40% Fewer Visitors Leaving Immediately

A one-person coaching business saw bounce rate drop sharply after load time was cut from 6 seconds to under 2. Visitors who previously left before the page finished loading now had time to read — and reach out.

// Example 03

First Page Locally Within 3 Weeks

A small trades business with no prior SEO ranked on the first page of local search results within three weeks of launch. Proper heading structure, location-relevant content, and fast load times did most of the work.

// Example 04

Better Leads, Less Back-and-Forth

A freelance agency added a structured intake form to their contact page. Pre-qualification emails dropped by more than half — enquiries now arrived with enough context to respond directly, cutting a 3-step exchange down to one.

// Example 05

Credibility That Closes the Gap

When a prospect is comparing options, your site decides whether they reach out at all. A solo consultant rebranding their offer found that a clear, fast, professional site did what a LinkedIn page couldn't — it signalled they were serious.

// Example 06

5+ Hours of Admin Saved Every Week

Connecting booking and notification systems to the site eliminated most manual coordination for a service business run by one person. That's more than half a working day returned — every single week.

* Illustrative examples based on typical patterns in this type of work. Actual results vary by business, market, and starting point.
